Hints for making good cards

  1. Choose question & answer based on the context in which you will need the information
  2. Don't give away (or suggest) the answer in the question
  3. Can add hint(s)
  4. Find a sweet spot of complexity - not too complex, not too stripped down
  5. Add context and examples. Copy & paste a bigger chunk with example and context, and highlight the most relevant part.
  6. Highlight most relevant parts / words
