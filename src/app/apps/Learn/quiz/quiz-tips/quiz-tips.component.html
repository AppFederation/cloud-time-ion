TIPS for quiz/reviewing
  1. Read out loud or in your head, to let it sink better
  2. Mark any part that is particularly important or difficult to remember
  3. Spend some time on each item, to reflect and let it sink in; but avoid getting stuck for too long (You will come back to it anyway!)
  4. Self-rate relative to required level of how precisely you want to learn it (Pareto principle)
  5. Remember that there are limits to what you can achieve with just memorizing, so make sure to spend time using the knowledge in practice.
